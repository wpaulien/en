Mobirise

Ceramics

LUCA School of Arts, in Ghent, Belgium, provides a serious and thorough program of the workshop: Glass and Ceramics. Within this workshop, I was taught a great deal about the history and techniques of both materials. 


The serene sensation that I experience when working with clay is unexplainable. The relationship between the material and myself is very special and intimate. The idea of having created something in clay and having gone through the whole process of creating that object, whether small or large, is a potent concept.
